This Privacy Policy describes how [Your Company Name] ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our School Bus Tracking App (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Student Information: Full name, school name, grade/class, pickup and drop-off locations
  • Parent/Guardian Information: Full name, contact phone number, email address
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of student boarding and alighting, bus route information, driver check-in/check-out times
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address (for technical support purposes)

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide real-time tracking of student transportation
  • To notify parents/guardians about student boarding and alighting
  • To maintain records of student transportation for school administration
  • To improve and maintain the App's functionality
  • To respond to support requests and technical issues
  • To ensure the safety and security of students during transportation

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • School Administrators: Designated school staff with proper authorization
  • Bus Drivers: Only for the specific routes they operate
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help operate our App (with strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

We do not sell student or parent information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure authentication protocols

5. Data Retention

We retain student and parent information:

  • For the duration of the student's enrollment in the school transportation program
  • For a reasonable period thereafter as required by school policies or applicable laws
  • Historical transportation records may be kept for administrative purposes

6. Parental Rights

Parents/guardians have the right to:

  • Review their child's information collected by the App
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information, subject to school record-keeping requirements
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Requests should be made through the school administration.
